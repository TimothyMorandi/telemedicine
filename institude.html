About Us

Situated in Die Boord, on the banks of the Eerste River, Stellenbosch in the Western Cape Province, Mediclinic Winelands Orthopaedic Hospital and the Institute of Orthopaedics and Rheumatology form a Specialist Institute, dedicated to surgical and medical management as well as rehabilitation of non-emergency musculoskeletal conditions, at the highest possible level.
